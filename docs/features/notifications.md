# Notifications and Emails in ClearHire

Notifications in ClearHire are the ways the app tells you about activity on your own account — in-app messages inside the product, optional push notifications on your device or browser, and a small set of transactional emails. All three are about your own account activity; none of them are marketing.

## In-app notifications

In-app notifications appear inside ClearHire and cover your own activity, including:

- **Application status** changes as you move through the stages in [application tracking](/features/applications-tracking).
- **Employment-verification** results when an employer confirms a role or a request times out — see [employment verification](/features/employment-verification).
- **Reviews** tied to your verified employment — see [company reviews](/features/company-reviews).
- **Admin announcements** — occasional product notices from the ClearHire team.

They are generated as these events happen and are visible whenever you are signed in.

## Push notifications (opt-in)

Push notifications are optional and **off by default**. When they are enabled for your ClearHire instance, you can turn them on for your device or browser in **Notification Settings**, and turn them off again at any time. ClearHire works fully without push — it is a convenience for hearing about activity while the app is closed, not a requirement.

## Transactional emails

ClearHire sends a few transactional emails tied to specific actions:

- **Welcome** email when you first sign in.
- **Contact-form reply** routing when you message the team.
- **Account-deletion confirmation** after you delete your account.
- **Identity-verification result** when your verification is decided.

ClearHire does **not** send marketing or promotional email, and there is no newsletter.

## What notifications do not do

- **They do not share your activity with anyone else.** Notifications are about your own account.
- **Push is not required.** Every feature works whether or not you enable push.
- **Emails are transactional only.** No marketing, no newsletter, no selling your address.

## FAQ

### How do I turn push notifications on or off?
Open **Notification Settings** in ClearHire and toggle push for your device or browser. It is off until you turn it on, and you can turn it off again at any time.

### Will ClearHire email me marketing?
No. ClearHire sends only transactional emails — welcome, contact-form reply, account-deletion confirmation, and identity-verification result.

### What can I get in-app notifications about?
Application-status changes, employment-verification results, reviews tied to your verified employment, and occasional admin announcements.

### Do I need push to use ClearHire?
No. Push is optional and off by default; the app is fully usable without it.
