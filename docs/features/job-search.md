# Job Search in ClearHire

Job search in ClearHire is the feature that helps you discover roles posted on the platform and narrow them with filters for location, remote status, salary, and skills, while saving searches and setting alerts so new matches reach you. It pairs with your [profile](/features/profile-management): the skills and experience you record make matching more relevant.

## Filtering your search

You can refine results by the criteria that matter most:

- **Location** — target specific cities or regions.
- **Remote** — focus on remote-friendly roles.
- **Salary** — filter by pay range where jobs include it.
- **Skills** — match roles to the [skills](/features/profile-management) on your profile.

Combining filters keeps the list focused. The more accurate your profile skills, the more useful skill-based filtering becomes.

## Saved searches

When you land on a filter combination that works, save it. A saved search remembers your criteria so you can re-run it in one tap instead of rebuilding filters each visit. This is ideal when you are tracking a niche — for example, "remote, React, senior" — over several weeks.

## Job alerts

Job alerts notify you when new roles match a saved search, so you do not have to check back constantly. Alerts turn job search from a chore into a feed: you set the criteria once and ClearHire surfaces fresh matches as they appear.

## From search to application

When you find a role worth pursuing:

1. Review the job details and requirements.
2. Tailor a CV in the [resume builder](/features/resume-builder) and check it with the [ATS checker](/guides/ats-resume-checker).
3. Apply, then log it in [application tracking](/features/applications-tracking) so you can follow it from applied to offer.

A [verified](/features/employment-verification) work history can strengthen your candidacy when you apply.

## What job search does not do

- **It does not aggregate the entire internet.** Results reflect jobs available within ClearHire, not every listing everywhere.
- **It does not guarantee matches or interviews.** Filters improve relevance; outcomes depend on open roles and employer decisions.
- **It does not auto-apply.** You decide where to apply; ClearHire helps you find and track roles.

## FAQ

### Can I filter for remote-only jobs?
Yes. Use the remote filter, optionally combined with location and skills, to focus on remote-friendly roles.

### How do saved searches differ from job alerts?
A saved search stores your filter criteria for quick re-running. A job alert builds on that by notifying you when new roles match those criteria.

### Why do my skills affect search results?
Skill-based filtering and relevance use the [skills](/features/profile-management) on your profile. Keeping them accurate improves which roles surface for you.

### Does ClearHire show salaries for every job?
Only when a job includes a salary range. Companies posting through [ClearHire for companies](/features/for-companies) can add ranges, which then feed the salary filter.

### What should I do after I apply?
Log the application in [application tracking](/features/applications-tracking) to monitor its stage, and consider requesting [employment verification](/features/employment-verification) for the roles a recruiter is most likely to scrutinize.
