# ClearHire Career Tools

ClearHire career tools are a set of features that help you prepare, position, and improve your job search beyond the resume itself — covering an ATS resume checker, cover-letter and bio generators, interview prep, salary insights, coding challenges, career paths, and LinkedIn and keyword optimizers. They operate on the data already in your [profile](/features/profile-management), and most of the processing runs client-side in your browser, so your inputs stay on your device while you work.

## The toolkit

| Tool | What it helps you do |
| --- | --- |
| ATS resume checker | Score a resume against a job description for keyword alignment |
| Cover-letter generator | Draft a tailored cover letter from your profile and a role |
| Bio / elevator-pitch generators | Produce a short professional bio or a spoken pitch |
| Interview prep | Practice with common questions and structure your answers |
| Salary insights | Get context on pay ranges to set expectations |
| Coding challenges | Practice technical problems for engineering roles |
| Career paths | Explore how roles and skills progress over time |
| LinkedIn optimizer | Improve your LinkedIn profile's clarity and keywords |
| Keyword optimizer | Align your wording with the language of target roles |

### ATS resume checker
Paste a job description and your resume to see how your keywords align. It is the single most actionable tool before applying. Full walkthrough: [ATS resume checker](/guides/ats-resume-checker).

### Cover-letter, bio, and pitch generators
These draft starting points from your real profile data — a cover letter for a specific role, a concise professional bio, or an elevator pitch. Treat the output as a first draft to refine in your own voice, not a finished, fact-checked document.

### Interview prep, salary insights, and coding challenges
Interview prep helps you rehearse and structure answers. Salary insights give context for negotiation and expectation-setting. Coding challenges let engineering candidates practice problems relevant to technical interviews.

### Career paths and optimizers
Career paths help you see where a role can lead and which skills bridge the gap. The LinkedIn and keyword optimizers sharpen how you present yourself, aligning your language with the roles you target — without keyword stuffing, which helps neither humans nor systems.

## Client-side processing

Most career tools run in the browser. That keeps interactions fast and means your inputs are processed on your device as you use them. It also sets honest expectations: these tools provide guidance based on your inputs, not access to any employer's private hiring system.

## How career tools fit your workflow

1. Build your [profile](/getting-started/create-your-profile) and a [resume](/features/resume-builder).
2. Find a role in [job search](/features/job-search).
3. Run the [ATS checker](/guides/ats-resume-checker), then draft a cover letter.
4. Prep for the interview, set salary expectations, and apply.
5. Log it in [application tracking](/features/applications-tracking).

## What career tools do not do

- **They do not guarantee results.** They are guidance, not a promise of interviews, offers, or specific salaries.
- **They do not fact-check generated text.** Always review drafts for accuracy and tone before sending.
- **They do not access employer systems.** Scores and insights are based on your inputs and general context, not a peek inside any company's ATS.

## FAQ

### Do the career tools cost anything?
No. All career tools are part of ClearHire, which has no subscriptions or in-app purchases.

### Does the cover-letter generator write the whole letter for me?
It drafts a tailored starting point from your profile and the role. You should refine it in your own voice and confirm every claim is accurate before sending.

### Is the salary insight specific to my city and role?
It offers context to help set expectations. Treat it as a reference point, not a precise quote for a specific employer.

### Do the tools send my data anywhere?
Most processing runs client-side in your browser. For the full picture of what ClearHire stores and which providers are used, see the [privacy summary](/privacy).

### Which tool should I use first?
The [ATS resume checker](/guides/ats-resume-checker) — aligning your resume to the job description is the highest-leverage step before you apply.
