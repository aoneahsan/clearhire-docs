# Gamification in ClearHire

Gamification in ClearHire is a set of motivational mechanics — badges, levels, streaks, and leaderboards — layered over your job search to encourage steady, consistent progress. Job hunting is a marathon that is easy to abandon; these mechanics give the routine work of updating your profile, building resumes, and applying a visible sense of momentum.

## The mechanics

| Mechanic | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| Badges | Recognize milestones and completed actions |
| Levels | Reflect ongoing engagement and progress |
| Streaks | Reward consistent, repeated activity |
| Leaderboards | Add light, optional comparison and friendly competition |

### Badges
Badges mark achievements — completing your [profile](/features/profile-management), building a resume, or hitting search milestones. They turn otherwise invisible progress into something you can see and build on.

### Levels
Levels rise with sustained activity, giving a longer-term signal of engagement than any single action. They reward showing up over weeks, not just one busy afternoon.

### Streaks
Streaks reward consistency — returning regularly to make progress. Because a job search benefits from steady effort, streaks nudge you to keep a useful cadence rather than cramming.

### Leaderboards
Leaderboards add an optional, light element of comparison. They are meant to motivate, not pressure; the real goal is your own progress, not outranking anyone.

## Why it's there

The purpose is behavioral, not cosmetic. Small, frequent wins make it easier to maintain the habits that actually move a job search forward: a current profile, tailored resumes, consistent applications, and follow-through in [application tracking](/features/applications-tracking).

## What gamification does not do

- **It does not change hiring outcomes.** Badges and levels reflect your activity in ClearHire; they are not seen by employers as qualifications.
- **It does not replace substance.** A high level means engagement, not a stronger candidacy on its own.
- **It is not a requirement.** You can ignore gamification entirely and use ClearHire's core features.

## FAQ

### Do employers see my badges or level?
No. Gamification reflects your engagement with ClearHire. It motivates you; it is not a credential shown to employers.

### How do I earn badges?
By completing meaningful actions — finishing your [profile](/getting-started/create-your-profile), building [resumes](/features/resume-builder), and progressing through your search. Specific badges follow the app.

### What happens if I break a streak?
Nothing punitive. A streak simply resets. The aim is to encourage consistency, not to penalize a missed day.

### Are leaderboards mandatory?
No. Leaderboards are an optional, light feature. ClearHire's value comes from its core tools, which you can use without engaging with competition.

### Does gamification affect my analytics?
Your [analytics](/features/analytics) track real activity like profile views and resume downloads. Gamification is a motivational layer on top of that activity, not a replacement for it.
