# Export or Delete Your ClearHire Data

Data export and deletion in ClearHire are the controls that let you take a copy of your information and permanently remove your account and data when you choose to. ClearHire stores your career data so the product can work across devices, and you remain in control of that data — you can export what you want to keep and delete the rest through the in-app flow.

## What ClearHire stores

Your account holds the data that powers the app, including:

- Your [profile](/features/profile-management) (experience, education, projects, skills, languages, certifications, awards)
- Resumes you build and related settings
- [Applications](/features/applications-tracking) you track and their stages
- [Reviews](/features/company-reviews) tied to verified employment
- [Verification](/features/employment-verification) requests and confirmations
- Files you upload (stored in FilesHub)

For the full list of what is stored and which providers are involved, see the [privacy summary](/privacy).

## Exporting your data

Before deleting anything, take copies of what you want to keep:

1. **Export your resumes** as [PDF or DOCX](/guides/export-resume-pdf-docx) — these are your most portable artifacts.
2. **Save important details** from your profile and tracked applications that you may want to reference later.
3. **Keep records** of any verified roles or reviews you care about.

Exported resume files live on your device and are unaffected by account deletion.

## Deleting your account and data

ClearHire provides an in-app account-deletion flow. To remove your account and associated data:

1. Open your account or settings area in the [web app](https://clearhire.aoneahsan.com) or [Android app](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aoneahsan.clearhire).
2. Find the **delete account** option in the account/privacy controls.
3. Confirm the deletion as prompted.

Deletion is significant: once your account and data are removed, the information in ClearHire is gone, so export anything you need first. Files uploaded to FilesHub are removed as part of the deletion of your data.

## Honest notes

- **Export does not happen automatically** when you delete — take your copies first.
- **Deletion is meant to be permanent.** Treat it as final rather than a pause.
- **Some records tied to other parties** (for example, a confirmation an employer made) follow ClearHire's data handling; the authoritative rules are in the in-app policy.

For the binding details on retention and your rights, read the in-app policy at [clearhire.aoneahsan.com/privacy](https://clearhire.aoneahsan.com/privacy), summarized in the [privacy overview](/privacy).

## FAQ

### How do I delete my account?
Use the in-app account-deletion flow in the account or settings area of the web or Android app, then confirm. Export anything you want to keep first.

### Will deleting my account remove my uploaded files?
Yes. Files stored in FilesHub are removed as part of deleting your data.

### Can I get my data back after deletion?
Deletion is intended to be permanent. There is no built-in undo, so export your resumes and important details beforehand.

### How do I keep a copy of my resumes?
Export them as [PDF or DOCX](/guides/export-resume-pdf-docx). Those files stay on your device after you delete your account.

### Where is the authoritative privacy policy?
In-app at [clearhire.aoneahsan.com/privacy](https://clearhire.aoneahsan.com/privacy). This docs site provides a plain-language [summary](/privacy), not the legal policy.
